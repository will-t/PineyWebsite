About Us

Address: 324 Piney Church Rd, Oakdale, TN 37829
Phone Number: 423-369-3612
Piney is a loving mission minded church. We have a wonderful choir and a very caring pastor. We offer many classes! We have Sunday school for all ages at 10:00am, and a breif childrens church just before the worship service that begins at 11:00am. Sunday evening we have training union at 5:00pm, the ladies meet and the men meet where both have personal study groups. The children have a lot of fun as well! Wednesday nights we have prayer meeting. What a blessing those prayer meetings are too!! Wednesdays the children and youth meet in the back with a variety of amazing mission classes both start at 7:00pm. We would love to have you, come visit us soon!! God bless you!
